My Favorite Coffee Spot

When I think of the perfect coffee spot, 'Camel Step' is the first name that pops into my head.

Why It's My Favorite?

here are some of the reasons why it's my favorite:
  • Incredible coffee
  • Great staff
  • High-quality coffee tools
  • Creative branding and logo
  • Beautiful interior

What to Order?

Camel Step offers a wide range of options, especially when it comes to espresso drinks, such as:
  • Espresso (obviously)
  • Flat White
  • Latte
  • Cortado and more

However, if espresso drinks aren't your thing, Camel Step also serves the best drip coffee,
including:
  • Coffee of the day
  • V60
  • Nitro Cold Brew
Personally, the iced V60 is my favorite. It has the perfect ice-to-coffee ratio,
and they’re consistently great with it. But if you’re not a fan of the strong V60 taste,
their coffee of the day won't disappoint.
